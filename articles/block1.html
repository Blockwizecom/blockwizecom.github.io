BITCOIN is the result of thinking differently. Satoshi Nakamoto's idea laid the groundwork for what's now a billion dollar industry. Bitcoin is a store of value, a currency allowing you to transact all over the globe... securely, quickly, cheaply and permits one to be their own bank. Okay okay... so why am i saying you shouldn't put a cent into it? Actually what i mean is bitcoin deserves more than a cent from you, this new tech is a big experiment thats made a lot of people start to look closer and question the current financial system. An experiment? yes i like to think of bitcoin sort of like a rent to buy on a car if we keep pulling through for bitcoin we will own something really big. So as much as there's this big vision of owning this really big Valuable bitcoin market bitcoin is already here and able to drive us daily, remittances, making payments for online services safer and quicker there's a lot more we could embrace bitcoin for and those possibilities will become a reality as more people enter the bitcoin space making more merchants start accepting bitcoin. You don't have to sell your house okay but do try put a couple of 10's and 100's into bitcoin and when you get opportunities to pay with bitcoin try paying with bitcoin, get used to paying differently. Bitcoin gives you freedom to decide what you yourself want do with it or the technology and way of thinking it's brought about.
