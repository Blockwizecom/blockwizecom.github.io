WHAT ON EARTH IS BITCOIN

Lets take a closer look at bitcoin:

 
       A Bitcoin is a digital asset of which only 21 million
       exactly like it will ever exist, They are securely kept
       in a decentralized network called a blockchain, no bitcoin
       ever leaves the blockchain and most importantly no fake 
       can enter this smart blockchain. Bitcoin can be sent from

        
 

 wallet to wallet securely and instantly within the blockchain
       anyone can have a wallet and even though all the bitcoins 
       and wallets are in this one place only the user with the 
       private key of a wallet with bitcoin in it can send or spend 
       bitcoins from that wallet, another thing about bitcoin is 

                           
 its quite anonymous even though the blockchain is transparent
       and we can see all transactions as they happen, your bitcoin
       wallet is not attached to your identity meaning when you 
       pay with bitcoin you're not risking someone getting a hold of 
       your private personal information. 

       
   Bitcoin (BTC) is traded on cryprocurrency exchanges where people can
       buy and sell Bitcoin for fiat currencies or other cryptocurrencies. 


       If you are still new to bitcoin here are some quick tips to get into bitcoin.

       
       1. Get a bitcoin Wallet app (blockchain wallet) is the most popular one, you
       could also use (coinomi).
 
       2. Both wallets above have a useful wallet backup feature that gives you words
       to write down and store in a safe place, those words can then be used to recover 
       your wallet and any funds in it in case you misplase or loose your mobile device.
       (recomend you to do this as soon as you download your wallet)
    
       3. Buying some bitcoin... Try localbitcoins.com you can find sellers in your own
       country who may use the same banks or payment systems you are familar with.
       You can also check out Paxful.com and have a wide variety of payment options from amazon
       gift cards to paypal.
  
       4. If you have a powerful computer and a reliable internet connection an uncapped one 
       would be better then you can try mining out the easy way. Go to minergate.com 
       register and download their miner software.

       5. Lastly beware of programmes and websites that promise high return on investment for 
       your bitcoins majority of them are scams. Do a lot af research and ask those who you 
       know an trust that have been in the bitcoin and crypto world for longer for help.
