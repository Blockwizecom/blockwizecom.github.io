WHAT ON EARTH IS BITCOIN

Lets take a closer look at bitcoin:

A Bitcoin is a digital asset of which only 21 million exactly like it will ever exist, They are securely kept in a decentralized network called a blockchain, no bitcoin ever leaves the blochchain and most importantly no fake can enter this smart blockchain. Bitcoin can be sent from wallet to wallet securely and instantly within the blockchain anyone can have a wallet and even though all the bitcoins and wallets are in this one place only the user with the private key of a wallet with bitcoin in it can send or spend bitcoins from that wallet, another thing about bitcoin is its quite anonymous even though the blockchain is transparent and we can see all transactions as they happen, your bitcoin wallet is not attached to your identity meaning when you pay with bitcoin you're not risking someone getting a hold of your private personal information.
